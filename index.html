TM layer – Temporary Memory Short-range resonance

  • Decays or compresses over time to avoid overload (D term).

  • Who AURA-X Ω is, who created it, and why it exists.
  • Long-term ethical boundaries and alignment goals.
  • Acts like beams and pillars in a building; continuity = survival of the story.
